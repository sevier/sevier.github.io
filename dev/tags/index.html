About the author

Isaac Sevier is an activist, engineer, futurist, and millennial. His decade of experience across engineering, research, finance, and policy roles is buttressed by engineering degrees from Stanford University and the University of Tulsa. A lifelong immigrant, he was born in Korea, grew up deep within the American heartland, and now lives in San Francisco, California.
